🥘 Deficit Bulk Prep Rules: Macro proteins and rice are cooked in advance. To preserve crunch and micronutrient density, all greens and vegetables are air-fried or boiled completely fresh right before you eat them.
Monday Batch Prep Session (~45 min | Covers Tue–Fri Macros)
  • Step 1 — Chicken Breast: Bake 2.5 lbs raw chicken at 375°F for 22–25 min. Slice and container into **151g cooked blocks** (Tue/Thu/Fri lunch).
  • Step 2 — 90% Lean Ground Beef: Brown 1.5 lbs in a pan, rinse in a colander to drain excess surface lipids. Separate into **140g cooked anchors** (Tue/Thu/Fri dinner).
  • Step 3 — Jasmine Rice: Cook 120g dry weight rice. Separate evenly into 3 lunch containers (~40g dry-equivalent weight per block).
  • ⚠️ Vegetable Fast Rule: Do NOT prep or steam broccoli or green strings ahead of time. Store them raw in crisper bins.
Friday Batch Prep Session (~45 min | Covers Sat–Mon Macros)
  • Step 1 — Chicken Breast: Bake 2.0 lbs raw chicken breast. Separate into **151g cooked blocks** (Sat/Mon lunch).
  • Step 2 — 90% Lean Ground Beef: Brown 1.0 lb. Separate into **140g cooked blocks** (Sat/Mon dinner).
  • Step 3 — Jasmine Rice: Cook 80g dry weight rice to cover Saturday and Monday lunch allocations.
